Location

Huntington Harbour
— Gilbert Island

Huntington Harbour serves as an expansive maritime sanctuary designed specifically for the yachting lifestyle, featuring a network of navigable waterways. The main channels are engineered to accommodate deep-draft vessels, complemented by generous bridge clearances that allow for easy passage to the open ocean. By maintaining extensive private docking and a strictly enforced no-wake zone, the Harbour ensures a secure and tranquil environment tailored for the maintenance and enjoyment of significant watercraft.
